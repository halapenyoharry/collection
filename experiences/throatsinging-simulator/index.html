About Throat Singing Styles

Khoomei: The fundamental style with balanced overtones, producing a soft, airy sound.

Sygyt: Emphasizes high, whistle-like overtones by constricting the throat and shaping the mouth cavity.

Kargyraa: Features deep, growling tones with prominent subharmonics, created by vibrating the false vocal folds.
